I am a senior software engineer at Moqi. Before that, I was a researcher at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab. I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. John C.S. Lui. I was a member of the Advanced Networking and System Research Laboratory (ANSRLab) at CUHK. I received my Bachelor degree in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2012, where I was a member of ACM Class.
