I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong starting from 2012. My supervisor is Prof. John C.S. Lui.

My research interests are mainly in distributed systems, parallel programing, machine learning, and algorithm design.

I received my Bachelor degree in Computer Science with honor from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I was in a special pilot computer science class, the ACM Honored Class, founded by Prof. Yong Yu. During my undergraduate, I was a research assistant in the Center for Brain-like Computing and Machine Intelligence, advised by Prof. Liqing Zhang.
